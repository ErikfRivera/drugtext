*The Social Effects of Punishment on Drug Users*

**Patricia G. Erickson** | © 1980 Addiction Research Foundation, Toronto | Foreword by John Hagan

*Cannabis Criminals* is a landmark 1980 study by sociologist Patricia G. Erickson examining what happens to cannabis users after they are arrested and convicted in Canada. Drawing on interviews with first-offenders in Metropolitan Toronto (1973–76), the book traces the social costs and benefits of criminalization — at the moment of sentencing and one year later — and argues for reform of Canadian drug law.
