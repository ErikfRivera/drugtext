*From [Rate Your Risks](/library/harm-reduction/rate-your-risks/), a harm-reduction manual published by the drugtext foundation (1999). Section 4.2.*

This chapter regularly mentions the word 'overdose'. What is an overdose? And what must be done in case of a heroin or cocaine overdose? Test your knowledge with this quiz.

*Note: This quiz originally appeared as an interactive form on drugtext.org. It is preserved here as static educational content.*

## Quiz Questions

- B. Incorrect

- B. Incorrect

- A. Correct
- B. Incorrect

**4. In case of a cocaine overdose, the user should best, and as quickly as possible, swallow sleeping pills or tranquillizers**

- A. Correct
- B. Incorrect

- A. Correct
- B. Incorrect

- B. Incorrect

- B. Incorrect
