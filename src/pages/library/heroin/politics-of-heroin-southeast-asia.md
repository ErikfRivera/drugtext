**The Politics of Heroin in Southeast Asia** by **Alfred W. McCoy**, with Cathleen B. Read and Leonard P. Adams II (Harper & Row, 1972) is the classic investigation of the heroin trade in Southeast Asia and its entanglement with the CIA, colonial opium monopolies, and the politics of the Cold War. This is the book's hub page; chapters are being republished individually and linked below as they become available.
