*By Andrew Preston, West Dorset community alcohol and drugs advisory service.*

*The Methadone Briefing* is a comprehensive clinical reference covering the pharmacology, legal framework, prescribing practice, dosing, detoxification, and special-population considerations for methadone treatment of opiate dependence. The full text is available here in seventeen sections.
